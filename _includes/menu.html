Menu

Le Trio will spark your interest with a mix of Vietnamese and American sandwiches and salads. With the exception of our freshly-baked pastries, all items are made to order.

We’re a collective of local chefs ready to bring the restaurant experience to the comfort of your home. Our multicourse dinners are perfect for 2 people and come ready to reheat. Multicultural menus & deli items change weekly and offer an array of flavors, professional techniques, and high-quality ingredients. We also offer for pick up different grocery items, deli meats, beer & wine. Check our Grocery store section! Like many of you, we’ve been impacted by current events and are excited to have this opportunity to continue to serve our neighborhood communities.
